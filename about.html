Who we are

ME Global Trading

Precision Engineering & Industrial Supply Solutions in UAE

ME Global Trading, established in the UAE, specializes in the supply of precision machining parts and custom-engineered industrial components for key sectors including manufacturing, oil & gas, defense, construction, infrastructure, and building materials. The company is also actively engaged in the trading and distribution of automotive spare parts, electric vehicles (EVs), and high-quality pre-owned vehicles, catering to the evolving demands of the global automotive market.

Quality, Reliability & Network

With a strong commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction, ME Global Trading connects trusted manufacturers and global suppliers with businesses and individuals seeking durable, high-performance products at competitive prices. Our extensive network ensures consistent availability, efficient sourcing, and seamless delivery of products across multiple industries.

Sustainable Mobility & Growth

Our mission is to support the growing demand for sustainable mobility solutions, while continuing to deliver premium automotive products, industrial components, and construction materials that meet international standards and market expectations.

Logistics & Distribution

As an affiliated company of ME Global Freight, a specialist in comprehensive freight and logistics solutions, we provide added value through streamlined supply chain management, efficient logistics support, and reliable global distribution services, ensuring timely and cost-effective delivery for our clients.
