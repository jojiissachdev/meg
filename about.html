ME Global Trading – Precision Engineering & Industrial Supply Solutions in UAE

Established in the UAE, ME Global Trading, specializes in the supply of precision machining parts and custom-engineered industrial components for sectors such as manufacturing, oil & gas, defense, construction, infrastructure, and building & other construction materials. The company is also actively engaged in the trading and distribution of automotive spare parts, electric vehicles (EVs) and used vehicles.

We provide high-quality industrial components in the UAE, ensuring durability, precision, and performance for a wide range of applications. Our expertise makes us a reliable partner for businesses seeking custom engineering solutions and industrial supplies.
